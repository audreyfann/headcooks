How it combines

  • Same ingredient, different words — “Baby spinach”, “fresh spinach”, “Spinach” all group together.
  • Same ingredient, different units — flour in cups and flour in grams are summed into one total, using standard staple densities.
  • Garlic is converted entirely into cloves, whatever unit it started in.
  • Nothing leaves your browser — the spreadsheet is parsed and combined locally; no upload, no server.

All processing happens in your browser. Values for garlic and staple densities are standard kitchen-reference approximations — good for shopping, not lab-precise.
